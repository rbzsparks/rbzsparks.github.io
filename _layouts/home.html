Who Am I?

Hi y'all! I'm Bobby, and I am a postbac psychology researcher at Stanford University. I am the current Lab Manager in the Language & Cognition Lab with Prof. Michael Frank. I graduated from Stanford in 2022 with a B.S. in Symbolic Systems concentrated in Cognitive Science. As an undergrad, I was an Honors Student and Undergrad RA in the Social Learning Lab with Prof. Hyowon Gweon and Aaron Chuey.

Research Interests

There is vast heterogeneity in developmental experience - How do we account for this variability in development? Broadly, I am interested in characterizing early variability and its effects on social, cognitive, and mental health outcomes. More specifically, I am interested in unpacking this heterogeneity to better understand 1) the ways children reason about & respond to variability, 2) the ways adversity interacts with development and 3) how we can differentially support development across diverse contexts. I aim to take an interdiplinary approach combining behavioral and computational methods from cognitive science, developmental science, education, and clinical science.
